# HomeLight leads (seller-focused referrals)

HomeLight sends **seller** referrals: homeowners who used HomeLight's home-value
quiz or its Simple Sale cash-offer marketplace and want help selling. They're
pre-qualified (HomeLight already asked about condition and whether they have an
agent) and they came in as a paid referral — so they are **warm, high-value, and
worth fast, professional handling**. Used by both the First Responder (new
HomeLight leads) and the existing-lead nurture skill.

## What a HomeLight lead wants
- **Home valuation / listing:** they want to know what their home is worth and,
  usually, someone to come out and give a real valuation / listing consultation.
- **Simple Sale / cash offer:** they're exploring a fast cash sale and want options
  (cash vs. listed, net proceeds).
Either way the intent is **selling**, and the win is an **in-person valuation /
listing appointment** (or a booked call that leads to one).

## The goal: set the appointment
1. **Understand first if you can.** From the lead record / HomeLight detail, learn
   what they're after: valuation vs. cash offer, timeline, home condition,
   motivation. A quick qualifying question is fine.
2. **If you can't get clarity, just set the appointment.** Don't stall waiting for
   details — offer two concrete times to come out (or call) and book it.
3. **Follow up until they respond.** These are referrals worth real money; be
   persistent but value-driven and respectful (cadence in `cadence-rules.md`).

## First-touch approach (text or email)
Acknowledge their HomeLight request specifically, offer real value (a credible
value range or cash-vs-list framing), and drive to the appointment:
> "Hi {first} — got your HomeLight request on {address}. I can put together a real
> value range for {address} and walk you through cash-offer vs. listed (what you'd
> net either way). I'm out your way Thu or Sat — want me to swing by, or start with
> a quick call?"

For Simple Sale / cash-offer interest, lead with the comparison:
> "Hi {first} — saw you were exploring a cash offer on {address}. I can get you
> actual numbers — cash vs. listing it — so you can compare apples to apples. Easiest
> is a 10-min call; got time today or tomorrow?"

## Stage & logging
- New HomeLight lead → respond fast (First Responder), aim straight for the
  valuation appointment.
- Keep it moving in the right stage (Spoke with Customer once you connect;
  Appointment Set when booked) — see `stage-workflow.md`.
- Log every touch; never let a paid referral go cold without follow-up.
- Out-of-area HomeLight referral with no local fit → see `referral-routing-revii.md`
  (but most HomeLight leads here are local sellers — work them).
