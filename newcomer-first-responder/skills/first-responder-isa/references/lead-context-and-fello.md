# Reading lead context & Fello inquiries (intent first)

A good message starts with a good read. Fello leads especially are **not** clean
"what's my home worth" records — you have to read the notes and history to learn
what the person actually wants. Never write before you've read.

## How Fello leads actually show up in FUB
Fello pushes activity into FUB; the meaningful detail usually lives in **notes and
fields, not an obvious headline**. Expect:
- The contact was **recently created or re-assigned** to the agent.
- One or more **notes / "inquiry" or "form summary" notes** describing what the
  lead did (a home-value request, a cash-offer request, landing-page or dashboard
  activity, campaign opens, a home-value *view*).
- **Fello custom fields** on the contact: estimated home value, equity position,
  last sold date, owner info, **Lead Score**, last engagement.
- Activity on the timeline (home-value views, email opens).

**So step one for any Fello lead: open and read the notes + Fello fields + the full
person history.** That's where the real interest is.

## The Fello lead types (know which one you're handling)
- **Home Value lead** — requested/viewed a valuation of a specific address.
- **Cash Offer lead** — asked about an instant/cash offer on their property.
- **Seller lead** — general seller-intent engagement.
- **Re-engaged buyer** — see the St. Augustine note below; this is common and easy
  to miss.

## The St. Augustine insight (do not miss this)
Many of these contacts are in the database because they **were or are buyers
interested in St. Augustine / St. Johns County** — that's how Newcomer originally
got their data. So a Fello "home value" ping is often a **warm re-engagement of a
known buyer/relocation prospect**, not a cold seller. Read the history: did they
previously search or inquire about St. Augustine-area homes? Are they active now?

This changes the play. The address they're valuing might be **out of state, yet
they want to BUY here** — that's a local relocation buyer, not a referral.

## Determine intent, then branch
For every Fello lead, work out intent from the notes/fields/history, then choose:

1. **Local home valuation (St. Augustine / St. Johns County address)**
   → Local seller/value play. Light touch acknowledging their interest, offer the
   real value + one driver, aim for a callback or listing consult.

2. **Out-of-area valuation BUT St. Augustine buy/relocation intent**
   (they own elsewhere, want to move/buy here — check history for prior St.
   Augustine buyer interest) → **Work locally as a relocation buyer.** Do **not**
   refer them away. Nurture toward a buyer consult / callback.

3. **Out-of-area valuation, no St. Augustine intent** (purely wants to sell/value
   there) → **Referral** via `referral-routing-revii.md`. You can still note you'd
   love to help if they ever consider St. Augustine.

4. **Ambiguous** → send one light, friendly qualifying question to surface intent
   before deciding (example in `value-message-library.md`). Don't assume.

## The goal for Fello leads
These leads usually just need a **thoughtful, light back-and-forth** — not a hard
sell. Be human, reference what they actually looked at, add one piece of value, and
steer gently toward a **callback or appointment**. A booked call/appointment is the
win, not a long text thread.

## What to read on every lead (Fello or not)
- **Notes** — especially Fello inquiry/form-summary notes.
- **Communication history** — texts, emails, the last open question.
- **Calls / dials** — attempts, connects, outcomes.
- **Property activity** — searches, views, favorites (strongest buying signal).
- **Stage, tags, source** — where they are and how they came in.
- **Fello fields** — Lead Score, value, equity, intent, last engagement.

## Segment, then choose the play
- **Buyer (local / relocation to St. Augustine)** → matching listings, area intel,
  buyer consult.
- **Seller / home-value (local)** → value + equity story, CMA, listing consult.
- **Out-of-area, no local intent** → referral (`referral-routing-revii.md`).
- **Investor** → numbers, cap rate, off-market angles.
- **Sphere / past client** → warm and relationship-first.
